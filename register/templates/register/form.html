Join leading Boston-area (and beyond!) workplaces by registering your organization to participate in the {% now "Y" %} Workplace Walk/Ride Day Challenge! It will kick off in April and wrap up in October.

How does it work?

  1. Businesses register below by April 15 (incentives for earlier registration) to let us know that they are participating.
  2. Employees check in their commutes once each month on the last Friday of the month to be counted and eligible for prizes.
  3. Winning companies are shown on live Leader Boards, segmented by size and sector. Individual winners are selected monthly from leading workplaces, and overall winning workplaces are recognized at an awards celebration, with special ice cream and fruit parties for winning companies by size.

What do you get with your registration?

  • Monthly online raffles, End of Year Winners' Parties, Awards Ceremony
  • Monthly results shown on Live Leader Board
  • Monthly staff check-in names and emails, along with disaggregated comments, and other data directly accessible to coordinators
  • Web and print materials explaining Challenge and FAQ's to staff
  • Coordinator support materials
  • Monthly newsletter and announcements to staff
  • Press releases to local media
  • Credit for Bike-Friendly Business status (Boston) and PTDM compliance (Cambridge).
  • Credit toward next year's fees for your organization's President/CEO signing this pledge to participate in this year's Challenge and participating. (10% credit for 100% participation, ie. checking in 7 times, and 5% credit for 70+% ie. Checking in 5 or 6 times). If you're eligible, just subtract the appropriate amount from the total fee. If you're not sure, send us an e-mail at info@gogreenstreets.org and we'll check.
